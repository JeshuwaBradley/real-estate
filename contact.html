Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you reach out

Here are a few common questions from buyers, sellers, and people exploring the platform.

How do I arrange a property visit?

You can send an inquiry through the property page or contact us directly. We will help coordinate the next steps with the relevant party.

Can I list my property with VeriEstate?

Yes. We can help review your property details, presentation requirements, and the process for being listed on the platform.

Do you provide legal or financial advice?

No. We help with clearer presentation and discovery, but independent legal and financial advice should still be obtained before any transaction.

How quickly do you respond?

Most general inquiries are answered within 24 hours, though urgent property requests may be handled faster.
